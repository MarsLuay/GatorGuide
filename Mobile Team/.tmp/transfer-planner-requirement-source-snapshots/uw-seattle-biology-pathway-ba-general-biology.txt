Apply to the Biology Major
Spring 2026 applications are due Friday, April 10th, by 11:59pm. If there are technical difficulties in regard to our website, please inform the advising office . We encourage people to apply well BEFORE close to the deadline. Please ensure that the application was actually submitted, and check that the offer email did not land in your spam/junk folder.
Admission to the BA and BS Degrees offered by the Department of Biology is by competitive application process. This process is designed not to limit access to the major but to assist students in careful planning and preparation for success in the Biology major. Students are encouraged to visit Biology advising early in their academic career or prior to transfer to begin planning for the major.
Applications are accepted quarterly. Applications received by the system after the quarterly cut off will be considered for the subsequent quarter.
Minimum application requirements for a Biology Major:
Be a matriculated student at the UW Seattle Campus and in good academic standing.
Complete the Introductory Biology series or equivalent courses to UW BIOL 180, 200, 220 and have a minimum grade of 2.0 in EACH course.
Have a minimum 2.5 Cumulative GPA for any supporting course work in Chemistry, Physics, Math, Biology or other courses intended for use in the Biology major that are complete at the time of application .
Meeting these minimum requirements will not guarantee admission to the Biology major. Other factors in admission include review of application questions, space availability in the major, and time to degree set by UW Satisfactory Progress Policy.
Application Deadlines:
Spring 2026 Open January 17th to April 10th
Summer 2026 Open April 11th to July 3rd
Autumn 2026 Open July 4th to October 9th
The Admissions Process:
Week 2: Applications close the 2nd Friday of the quarter and then reviewed after that date.
Week 3: Application review is completed and offer emails are sent by end of day on the 3rd Friday of the quarter to the UW address of the applicant.
Week 4: Once offer emails are sent, the student has 7 days to click the survey link and accept the offer. Please check that the offer email did not land in your spam/junk folder . Students who do not accept the offer by the deadline ( 4th Friday of the quarter ) indicated in the offer email will need to reapply in a subsequent quarter (if eligible). After you accept the offer, you will be sent to a confirmation page once your submission is completed. Additionally, you can re-click on the submission offer link to see if it was confirmed.
Students who are not offered admission will receive a notification email and are encouraged to speak with Biology Advisers prior to reapplying.
Week 5: Students who are not already declared in another major will be declared in the system in time to remove major-related holds for the next quarter's registration as pending for the following quarter. This happens about a week or two after the deadline for the offer.
Week 5/6: Orientation dates will be sent out on a regular quarterly basis. Summer new admits will be included in the Autumn orientation cycle
We strongly encourage students who do not meet the minimum application requirements to meet with a Department of Biology Academic Adviser to discuss their options.
