Bachelor of Science with a Major in Marine Biology Degree Requirements
Use the following tables to plan for a Major in Marine Biology*. Current, as well as prospective UW students are invited to use the MyPlan online academic planning tool to create a full academic plan over quarters and years. In addition to the tables below, students can also download or print the following requirements worksheet:
Marine Biology Major Requirements Worksheet
Sample Schedules & Academic Planning
The worksheet and tables are good for broad academic planning, but course offerings vary by quarter and year. To view the detailed quarterly list of courses relevant to our program, please visit the Marine Biology Courses page.
Detailed List of Marine Biology Courses by Quarter
General Education requirements
Students are required to complete the general education requirements of the College of the Environment. Some of the credits applied to these requirements may also fulfill major requirements.
College of the Environment General Education Requirements
In addition to the general education requirements, a UW student earning any Bachelor&#8217;s degree must meet the following University requirements:
Earn a total minimum of 180 quarter credits
A minimum of 45 credits taken as a matriculated University of Washington student ( a minimum of 45 of your final 60 credits must be taken at the UW )
A minimum cumulative GPA of 2.0

Electives
Students develop content knowledge and skills through a large selection of courses offered at the upper-division level through the School of Aquatic & Fishery Sciences, the School of Oceanography, the Department of Biology, and the Friday Harbor Laboratories. Students complete electives from the department&#8217;s approved list to meet the following requirements:
Elective credit requirements
Minimum of 25 credits total from the department&#8217;s approved list
Category requirements
Biodiversity (1 course minimum)
Ecology and Ecosystems (1 course minimum)
Organismal Processes (1 course minimum)
Additional elective courses to bring total credits to 25
Additional requirements
Minimum of 3 courses at the 400-level
Minimum of 2 courses taken with lab sections
No more than 6 credits total of independent research or internship
Marine Biology Elective List
Integrative Field Experience
Culminating requirement of the major, which has all students spending at least three weeks in the field away from the Seattle Campus. Most options involve studying in residence at Friday Harbor Labs , the UW&#8217;s marine field station on San Juan Island. See the page below for details on the requirement and eligible courses.
Integrative Field Experience requirement
Minimum 6 credits from the approved list
Marine Biology Integrative Field Experience
