Declare Healthcare Leadership Major
Main Content
Major prerequisites
Students interested in declaring Healthcare Leadership as their major must meet the following requirements prior to enrolling:
Must be admitted to UW Tacoma
A cumulative GPA of at least 2.0 on a 4.0 scale in all college course work
A minimum 60 quarter credits completed
5 credits of English composition (10 additional writing-intensive credits completed via required Healthcare Leadership course work)
Currently licensed Washington State healthcare providers must provide proof of unrestricted license status (does not apply to non-licensed applicants)
Agreement to adhere to the Essential Behaviors for Admission, Continuation and Graduation and Social Networking Policy.
Application Steps
TRANSFER STUDENT
Apply to UW-Tacoma as a Transfer Student .
Be admitted to UW Tacoma
Meet with our academic advisor to declare/change your major. Students admitted to UW Tacoma can declare anytime.
Begin BAHCL program autumn quarter.
CURRENT UW TACOMA STUDENT
Meet with our academic advisor to declare/change your major at anytime.
Begin BAHCL program autumn quarter.
Returning students and international students should contact the academic advisor: Adrianna Plavetsky, amp93@uw.edu , for more information.
