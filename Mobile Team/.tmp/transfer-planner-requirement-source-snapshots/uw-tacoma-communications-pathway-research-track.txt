Degree Requirements
You need to complete 55 credits, which must include a minimum of 20 credits of upper-division (300–400 level) courses.
Foundation (10 credits)
TWRT 211 Argument and Research in Writing AND one of the following theory and methods courses:
TCOM 444 Gender, Ethnicity, Class and Media
TCOM 453 Critical Approaches to Mass Communication

Optional Capstone (5 credits)
Research Track students may choose to complete a 5-credit, 25–35 page senior thesis (as part of the Core credits). Ideally, you would have completed all Core and Foundation courses before undertaking the thesis. The thesis must be supervised by Core Communication faculty. This means early planning is crucial, and you should develop a rapport with your supervisor by the time you reach the thesis stage.
