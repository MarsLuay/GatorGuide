Computer Science & Software Engineering
Major Planning Worksheet
&#xA0;
The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Software Engineering (CSSE) &#xA0;focuses on computer programming and human-centered software development processes. Students gain essential knowledge in object-oriented programming, data structures, analysis of algorithms, software engineering, management principles, hardware architecture, and operating systems.
UNIVERSITY MINIMUM ADMISSIONS REQUIREMENTS
Transfer applicants must meet the following minimum requirements to be considered for admissions:
Minimum cumulative transfer GPA of 2.0.
Supporting documents (transcripts, test scores, personal statement, etc. &#x2013; varies by major program).
Completion of CADR or core subject requirements if applicant has fewer than 40 transferrable credits (see below) at the time of application.
Proof of&#xA0; English language proficiency &#xA0;(if required) .
MAJOR ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS
This is a capacity-constrained major , meaning that students who meet the general admission requirements and minimum GPA in each prerequisite course are eligible for review; however, admission is limited by the number of seats available in the program and is not guaranteed. &#xA0;

COURSE EVALUATIONS
To determine if your coursework is equivalent to the prerequisites listed above, we encourage you to submit this form with your unofficial college transcripts and our transfer admissions counselor assigned to this major will follow up with you. You can also reference the&#xA0; UW Equivalency Guide &#xA0;which lists course equivalencies for Washington State Community & Technical Colleges, but if you have issues understanding it, please either submit this form or connect with your&#xA0; transfer admissions counselor .
PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS
Minimum 2.00 GPA in each prerequisite course
All prerequisites completed by the application deadline
Please note: Minimum grades in prerequisite courses do not guarantee admission. Refer below to competitive applicant criteria.
COMPETITIVE APPLICANTS
Applicants who are generally competitive to CSSE will have the following:&#xA0;
Prerequisite GPA of 3.50
Cumulative college GPA of 3.50
A well-written personal statement&#xA0;
&#x200B;&#x200B;&#x200B;&#x200B; A positive grade trend with few to no repeats
POST-BACC/GRADUATE APPLICANTS
Since the program prioritizes applicants without Bachelor's degrees, it is rare for students already holding a completed Bachelor's degree to be admitted to the program. We recommend that students interested in a Computer Science degree at UW Bothell who already hold a Bachelor's degree explore our Graduate Certificate in Software Design and Development .
