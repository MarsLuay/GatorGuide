# Course Planner Majors and Degree Requirements

Last updated: March 30, 2026

This doc is the source-backed inventory for every major currently supported by the GRC -> UW course planner.

Important note:

- "Every single course needed to graduate" does not always mean one fixed list.
- Several UW degrees use approved core-course pools, capstone pools, technical-elective pools, or option lists.
- In those cases, this doc records:
  - the fixed named courses
  - the required bucket counts
  - the official page or PDF that defines the full approved list
- When the current planner only has transfer-prep coverage, that is called out clearly instead of guessing a full audit.
