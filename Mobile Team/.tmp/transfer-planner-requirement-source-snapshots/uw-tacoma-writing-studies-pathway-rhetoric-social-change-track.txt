Degree Requirements
To complete a BA in the Rhetoric, Writing and Social Change track of Writing Studies, you need to complete 60 credits, including 45 credits in upper-division (300–400 level) courses, with a minimum 2.0 GPA.
Foundation (10 credits)
TWRT 211 Argument and Research in Writing AND
TWRT 388 Writing for Social Change
Rhetoric and Writing Electives (25 credits)
These courses can only be taken once as either Rhetoric and Writing Electives OR as Social Change Electives.
TWRT 320 Rhetoric, Public Life and Civic Engagement
TWRT 330 Written and Visual Rhetoric
TWRT 333 Writing through Comics
TWRT 340 Asian American Rhetorics, Literacies and Activism
TWRT 353 UX Writing
TWRT 364 Food Writing for Cultural Exploration
TWRT 420 Rhetoric and the City: Composing Urban Landscapes
TWRT 440 Cross Cultural Communication Design
