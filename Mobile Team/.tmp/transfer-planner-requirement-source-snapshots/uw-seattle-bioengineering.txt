Once completing the B.S., the following summer is usually spent researching, and the fifth year is spent on coursework, research, writing and thesis defense.
Once enrolled, Bioengineering PhD students have the opportunity to pursue their PhD with a Data Science option. The Data Science option prepares the next generation of thought leaders to both apply new data science methods and build new data science tools. It recognizes Bioengineering Ph.D. students whose thesis work focuses specifically on advanced data science tools and provides an advanced education to students who will push the state-of-the-art in data science methods, such as developing new machine learning methods.
