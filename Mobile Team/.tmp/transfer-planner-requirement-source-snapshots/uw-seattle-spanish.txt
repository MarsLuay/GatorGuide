Our major offers courses that enable students to achieve advanced proficiency by understanding the literary and cultural traditions of Spain, Latin America, and the Latinx US. Undergraduates will be prepared to conduct meaningful and creative analysis in the field and apply their linguistic skills and cultural sensibility to various career paths. We also offer a Honors Program .

The Spanish major is a minimum prerequisite major . Students who meet the prerequisites will be admitted, provided they can complete the major requirements within the UW's academic satisfactory progress policy. Applications to the Spanish major are accepted year-round.
The United States, with the second largest Spanish-speaking community in the world (only behind Mexico), is heading towards a future where almost one-third of its population will be fluent by 2050. Whether because of the excellent career advancement possibilities or an interest in the vibrant cultures behind it, learning Spanish will open doors to exciting new places.
