Degree Requirements
To complete a BA in the Technical Communication track of Writing Studies, you need to complete 65 credits, including 45 credits in upper-division (300–400 level) courses, with a minimum 2.0 GPA.
Foundation (10 credits)
One required course:
TWRT 291 Technical Communication in the Workplace
5 credits from the following list: (These courses can only be taken once, as either Track Foundational Courses or Electives.)
TWRT 331 Writing in the Natural Sciences
TWRT 350 Principles of User Centered Design
TWRT 391 Advanced Technical Communication
TCOM 320 Principles of Web Design
