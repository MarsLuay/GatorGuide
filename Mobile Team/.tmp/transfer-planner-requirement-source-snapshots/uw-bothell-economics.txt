Curriculum
Bachelor of Science in Economics degree
Degree Requirements
Students pursuing the B.S. in Economics major must complete the following 55 credits for graduation.
Course Type
Course Numbers & Names
Core Courses ( prerequisites in parentheses)
30 credits:
B BUS220/BIS 200: Principles of Microeconomics
B BUS 221/BIS 201: Principles of Macroeconomics (BBUS 220, or BIS 200, or B CUSP 200)
B BUS 210: Financial Accounting
B BECN 302: Intermediate Microeconomics (BBUS 220, or BIS 200 and STMATH 114, or 124, or 125, or 126, or B MATH 144 )
B BECN 303: Intermediate Macroeconomics (B BECN 302)
B BECN 382: Introduction to Econometrics (B BECN 302)
Economics Electives (prerequisites in parentheses)
20 Credits from the following list:
B BECN 300: Quantitative Methods in Economics (STMATH 114, or 124, or 125, or 126, or B MATH 144)
B BUS 301: Data Management ( BIS 215, or BBUS 215, or B MATH 215, or STMATH 341)
B BECN/B BUS 458: Risk Modeling (B BECN 302)
B BECN 460: Financial Economics ( BECN 302 and either B BECN 458, or STMATH 330 and STMATH 392)
B BECN 469: Game Theory (BBUS 310 or STMATH 114, or 124, or 125, or 126, or B MATH 144)
General Electives (any 300/400 level B BECN or B BUS course)
5 credits
Total Credits
55 Credits
General Education Requirements
In addition to the B.S. in Economics course requirements, students are required to fulfill the UW Bothell general education requirements.
● English Composition (C) – 5 credits (A grade of 2.0 is required)
● Additional Writing (W) – 10 credits
● Reasoning (RSN) – 5 credits
● Natural Sciences (NSc) – 15 credits
● Arts and Humanities (A&H) – 15 credits
● Social Sciences (SSc) – 15 credits
● Diversity (DIV) – 5 credits (3 credits for students admitted to UWB prior to Fall 2023)
*Note: The DIV credits can overlap with General Education requirements above.
