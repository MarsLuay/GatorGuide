All prospective SBSE students should be aware that while students can join the major at any time, the SBSE-prefixed courses begin in Autumn Quarter of Junior Year (year 3). Students admitted prior to this need to be completing all of the courses included in years 1 and 2 of the SBSE academic plan so they are ready to start the SBSE-prefixed courses in Autumn of year 3. There are two years (6 quarters, Autumn-Spring) of required SBSE-prefixed courses.

Current BSE majors are either starting year 3 (Junior year) or year 4 (Senior year) of the major. While all student situations are different, by and large, most Seniors will likely find it advantageous to stay in the BSE major, whereas many Juniors will likely find it advantageous to switch to the SBSE major. In the end though, it comes down to which set of requirements you would prefer to complete and which major title you would prefer. Please reach out to SEFS Advising if you have specific questions about your particular situation.
Current BSE Majors who know they wish to switch to the SBSE curriculum can do so by emailing a request to be switched to SEFS Advising .

Students
Students admitted after Autumn quarter of Junior year (year 3) will need to be working on other courses required for the major and begin the SBSE courses in the following Autumn quarter. SBSE course content builds from quarter to quarter, with completion of previous courses a prerequisite for enrolling in courses in the following quarter, so beginning the first quarter of SBSE-prefixed courses in Autumn quarter of year 3 is required.
